comments: jin fu is a good substitution if i'm craving hot pot but don't want to go all the way to kpot although i do think i still prefer kpot for hot pot just because i like the variety of the sauce bar and base flavors there. jin fu doesn't have that many sauce bar variations and i found there base soups to be limiting and not appealing to my usual tastes. but i think it's fine it's not a deal breaker. also there is this one beef cut that was super fatty and melty in ur mouth but i unfortunately do not remember it. i would say good portions for each add-in tho. a bit pricey but would go again.
