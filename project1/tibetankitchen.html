# of times i have visited: 1 time


dishes i have ordered: thukpa dhrangmo (spicy cold noodles w. sesame oil dressing), dhara (mango yogurt shake), tingmo (tibetan steamed bun), phaksha shaptak (twice cooked pork belly), sha momo (deep/pan fried dumplings)


my recs: phaksha shaptak, thukpa dhrangmo, tingmo


comments: heard good things so tried this place a few weeks ago. would say it lived up to the hype. went on a weekday evening and it's packed. this place is always packed so maybe go during early dinner or way later. personally though the momos were a bit doughy for me and the tibetan bread is kinda plain by itself but the tibetan bread pairs well with the slightly spicy pork belly dish.
